How to Use

Two layers, colour-coded. The Weekend Escaper and Breadfast Loyalist events (plus gold "Both / owned") are the strategic layer. The grey dashed items are your existing campaigns and scheduled posts, carried forward at their real cadence. Use the filter chips to isolate the new work from what's already running.

Every item has a concrete example. Click a day to set channel, asset type, organic/paid, language, objective, publish time, budget, a short calendar label, and an example / copy field (the actual SMS text, the caption, or the in-app operation). Hover a calendar chip to preview its metadata.

The plan is a sequence. In-App + SMS run all six months at $0 (banner, AI booking entry and the weekly newsletter are content-only). Search gets a capped paid pilot (M2–M4). Social is an organic-only test on one platform (Instagram, M4–M5), led by the four brand pillars. Month 6 is a decision gate.

Adjustable references. The Budget tables and the four brand pillars can all be changed and persist in this browser. Use Export/Import .json to share a version with the team.

The timing rule: bookings peak Thursday (marked ★). Conversion content runs Wed–Thu 7–9pm; brand-story runs Fri–Sun.
