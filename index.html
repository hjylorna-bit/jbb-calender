How to Use

Purpose: a visual content planner across all JbB channels for 6 months, built around two priority segments and the platform's real booking rhythm.

Priority segments: Seg 1 (Weekend Escapers — volume) and Seg 3 (Family Seekers — value). Seg 5 (Breadfast Loyalists) is the acquisition channel that carries both, threaded through owned channels.

The timing rule: bookings peak Thursday (pre-weekend in Egypt). Promo/conversion content runs Wed–Thu; brand-story content runs Fri–Sun. Send SMS/push 7–9pm. Thursdays are marked with a star ★ on the grid.

Content direction: evolve from generic memes to branded storytelling anchored to real destinations and real EGP prices. Default to domestic (Hurghada, Sharm, North Coast) and EGP.

Editing: click any day to open it, add or edit items, choose a channel and segment for each. Use the segment filter to focus the view. Changes auto-save in this browser; use Export/Import .json to share with the team.
